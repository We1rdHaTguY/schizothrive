What's happenin'? You can call me J Man

I'm diagnosed with schizoaffective disorder bipolar type, bipolar disorder, major depression, and general anxiety disorder. I've visited a psychiatric facility seven times, six of those times against my will. I decided to make this blog as a way to express my creativity while bringing advice and inspiration to those who have suffered from similar afflictions.
