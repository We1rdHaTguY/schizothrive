Welcome!

What's happenin'?

You can call me J Man. I'm a diagnosed schizoaffective, bipolar, and major depressive with general anxiety disorder (it's a mouthful, I know). I've visited a psychiatric facility seven times, six of those times against my will. I decided to create this blog as a way to express my creativity while bringing advice and inspiration to those who have suffered from similar afflictions with the hope that my story—and how I keep myself motivated to achieve—reaches those like me who need it most.
